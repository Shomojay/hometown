The Oshodi transport interchange is a world-class bus terminal in Oshodi. It is Nigeria's and West Africa's largest bus terminal.

Oshodi Art Gallery is a creative venture that has culture and tourism content. It is tourist center that value and promote Nigeria art and cultural heritage

Night Life

The Builders Lounge and Bar is a great place to unwind and have a good time. With its modern style and relaxing atmosphere, you can eat, drink, and enjoy the occasional Karaoke and comedy night.
